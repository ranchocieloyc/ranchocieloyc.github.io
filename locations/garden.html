Complete with 47 raised beds, a variety of fruit trees, composting bins, a koi fountain and a green house, the Leadership Garden is a beautiful and thriving part of the Rancho Cielo campus.
Here the students take weekly Gardening Classes and engage with guest speakers from places like Earthbound Farms and other local agriculture businesses. Eventually, it is our hope that the Culinary Students can come to the Garden to gather herbs and vegetables for their recipes!
