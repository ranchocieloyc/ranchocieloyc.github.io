Our Construction Academy offers on-the-job training and mentorship in all aspects of Construction. Co-enrolled as Hartnell Students, Rancho Cielo students assist in constructing residential homes from start to finish, complete a wide variety of construction projects such as roofing, framing, foundation, finish carpentry, decking and concrete, and receive instruction on the proper use and handling of all hand tools (power tools, tape measures, skill saws, table saws,
jig saws, chop saws,band saws, rotary hammers, jack hammers, and power drills). Students learn how to construct tables, chairs, wine racks, bird houses, and more! Currently, they are learning about solar energy and working with solar professional to install solar panels
